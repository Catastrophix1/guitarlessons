Meet Ben

Professional musician and instructor with over 15 years in the music industry.

Born in London, I've had the privilege of experiencing music at the highest level. As frontman of Alien Breed (Universal Records), we reached #13 on the US Billboard Modern Rock chart and toured internationally.

I've performed at major festivals including Coachella, SXSW, X-Games and Bluetorch, and supported artists like Foo Fighters, Incubus, Chevelle and The Fun Lovin' Criminals.

Alongside my performing career, I've taught over 1,000 students in private lessons and group settings at my north London studio.

"My goal is to help you make the quickest progress possible while having a lot of fun along the way."

What I Teach

All styles, all levels - from complete beginner to advanced

Acoustic Guitar

From fingerpicking to strumming, learn to play your favourite songs and develop your own style.

Electric Guitar

Rock, blues, metal, funk - master techniques from bending and vibrato to shredding solos.

Bass Guitar

Lock in with the rhythm section. Learn grooves, slap technique, and how to hold down the low end.

Ukulele

The perfect instrument to get started. Fun, portable, and easier to learn than guitar.

Songwriting & Singing

Write your own music and learn to accompany yourself. Express yourself through original songs.

Music Production

Learn Ableton Live, Studio One, and DJing. Record, produce, and release your own music.
